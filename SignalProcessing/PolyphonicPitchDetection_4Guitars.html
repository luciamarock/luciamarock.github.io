The Polyphic Pitch Detection Algorithm for Guitars is something I developed at the time of my collaboration with Benti (a Swizz start-up), since then I have evolved the algorithm but these upgrases are not yet public.

Anyway the first version of the algorithm was based solely upon two techniques: the RTFI and the classic FFT.

I have published separately these two techniques and their implementation is available on my GituHub:

  • RTFI technique
  • FFT (personal implementation)

When putting everything together you obtain the algorithm I have developed for Benti (which is not public) at the moment.
