Silencio Desaparecido

Electric Guitar and Electronics Composition

Silencio Desaparecido is a contemporary composition by Luciano Ciamarone originally written for string quartet and electronics and later reworked for electric guitar and electronics due to performance constraints.

This transformation reflects a compositional approach where instrumental identity is flexible, allowing musical material to migrate between ensembles and electroacoustic settings.
