Aesthetica - Sound Installation at Parco Degli Acquedotti (Rome)

Located at the Parco degli Acquedotti in Rome, this installation explores the intersection of acoustic physics and quantum theory. The work utilizes acoustic convolution to merge the site’s natural Impulse Responses (IR) with synthetic audio generated through algorithms based on quantum probability and wave interference.
